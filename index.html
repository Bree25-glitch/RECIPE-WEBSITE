Breakfast Recipes

Start your morning with delicious homemade meals

Easy

Fluffy Pancakes

20 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  2. Mix eggs, milk, and melted butter
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  4. Heat pan with butter
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake
  6. Cook until bubbles form, flip
  7. Serve with syrup and fruit
Quick

Baked Eggs

15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • Salt and pepper
  • Herbs (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Grease baking dish
  3. Crack eggs into dish
  4. Pour milk around eggs
  5. Add cheese and tomato
  6. Bake 12-15 minutes
  7. Serve with toast
Easy

French Toast

15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon
  2. Dip bread slices in mixture
  3. Heat butter in pan
  4. Cook until golden both sides
  5. Serve with honey or syrup
Healthy

Fruit Smoothie

5 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup milk or yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Adjust consistency with milk
  4. Pour into glass
  5. Serve immediately

Easy

Fluffy Pancakes

20 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1.5 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Whisk dry ingredients together.
  2. Combine wet ingredients and mix with dry.
  3. Cook on a buttered pan until golden.
Quick

Baked Eggs

15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Crack eggs into ramekins, add milk and toppings.
  2. Bake at 180°C for 12–15 minutes.
Easy

French Toast

15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Dip bread in egg mixture and fry in butter until golden.
Healthy

Fruit Smoothie

5 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup milk or yogurt

Instructions:

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth and serve chilled.

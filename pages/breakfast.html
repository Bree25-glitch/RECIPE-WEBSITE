Breakfast Recipes

Start your morning with delicious homemade meals.

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons butter

How To Cook

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add eggs, milk, and butter.
  3. Whisk until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour batter and cook both sides until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with syrup or fruits.

Baked Eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup cheese
  • 1 tomato
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

How To Cook

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  2. Crack eggs into a baking dish.
  3. Add tomatoes and cheese.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 15 minutes.
  6. Serve warm.

French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 bread slices
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Butter

How To Cook

  1. Mix eggs, milk, and sugar.
  2. Dip bread slices into mixture.
  3. Heat butter in a frying pan.
  4. Cook bread until golden brown.
  5. Flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve warm with honey.

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

How To Cook

  1. Add bananas and strawberries into blender.
  2. Pour in milk and honey.
  3. Add ice cubes.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into glass and serve chilled.

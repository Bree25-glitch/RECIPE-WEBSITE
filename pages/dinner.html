Comforting dinners to end the day on a high note.

Ugali with Sukuma Wiki

40 mins

Ingredients:

  • Maize flour
  • Sukuma wiki (kale)
  • Onion and oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare ugali by cooking maize flour with water until thick.
  2. Sauté sukuma wiki with onion and serve together.

Pilau

60 mins

Ingredients:

  • Rice, meat, pilau spice

Instructions:

  1. Sauté spices and meat, add rice and cook until tender.

Chapati with Beans

45 mins

Ingredients:

  • Flour, water, cooked beans, onions, tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Make chapati dough, cook on pan and serve with bean stew.

Nyama Choma (Grilled Meat)

50 mins

Ingredients:

  • Meat, garlic, lemon, salt

Instructions:

  1. Marinate meat and grill until cooked through.

Vegetable Rice

30 mins

Ingredients:

  • Rice, carrots, peas, corn, onions

Instructions:

  1. Cook vegetables, add rice and simmer until tender.

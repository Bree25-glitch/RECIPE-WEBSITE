Dinner Recipes

End your day with delicious and satisfying dinner dishes.

1. Ugali with Sukuma Wiki

Ingredients:

  • Maize flour
  • Water
  • Sukuma wiki (kale)
  • Onion
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

How to Cook:

  1. Boil water and slowly add maize flour while stirring.
  2. Cook until it becomes thick ugali.
  3. Fry onions in oil.
  4. Add sukuma wiki and cook until soft.

2. Pilau

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Beef or chicken
  • Pilau masala
  • Onion
  • Garlic & ginger

How to Cook:

  1. Fry onions until brown.
  2. Add meat and cook well.
  3. Add spices and mix.
  4. Add rice and water, then simmer until cooked.

3. Chapati with Beans

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Beans
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Cooking oil

How to Cook:

  1. Make chapati dough and roll flat circles.
  2. Cook on a hot pan with oil.
  3. Boil beans until soft.
  4. Fry onions and tomatoes, then add beans.

4. Nyama Choma (Grilled Meat)

Ingredients:

  • Goat or beef meat
  • Salt
  • Lemon
  • Garlic

How to Cook:

  1. Season meat with salt, lemon, and garlic.
  2. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill over charcoal until well cooked.

5. Vegetable Rice

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Corn
  • Onion

How to Cook:

  1. Boil rice until half cooked.
  2. Fry onions and vegetables.
  3. Add rice and mix well.
  4. Steam until ready.

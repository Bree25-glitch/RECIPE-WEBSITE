Supper Recipes

Light and satisfying supper ideas.

Grilled Chicken with Ugali

50 mins

Ingredients:

  • Whole chicken
  • Maize flour
  • Onions and tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Season and grill chicken; prepare ugali and serve together.

Beef Stew with Rice

90 mins

Ingredients:

  • Beef, rice, onions, tomatoes

Instructions:

  1. Brown beef, simmer with tomatoes until tender, and serve with rice.

Fried Fish with Chips

30 mins

Ingredients:

  • Fish fillets, potatoes, oil

Instructions:

  1. Fry chips until crispy and fry seasoned fish; serve with lemon.

Spaghetti with Minced Meat

30 mins

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti, minced meat, tomato sauce

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti and simmer meat sauce; combine and serve.

Chapati with Beans Stew

45 mins

Ingredients:

  • Flour, cooked beans, tomatoes, onions

Instructions:

  1. Cook chapatis and serve with a hearty beans stew.

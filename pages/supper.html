Supper Recipes

Discover delicious and easy-to-make supper recipes for every day of the week.

Grilled Chicken with Ugali

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 cups maize flour
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

How to Cook

  1. Season the chicken.
  2. Grill until cooked.
  3. Prepare ugali.
  4. Serve hot.

Beef Stew with Rice

Ingredients

  • 500g beef
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Salt

How to Cook

  1. Boil beef until tender.
  2. Cook onions and tomatoes.
  3. Add beef and spices.
  4. Serve with rice.

Fried Fish with Chips

Ingredients

  • 2 whole fish
  • 4 potatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt

How to Cook

  1. Season and fry fish.
  2. Cut potatoes into chips.
  3. Deep fry until crispy.
  4. Serve together.

Spaghetti with Minced Meat

Ingredients

  • 1 packet spaghetti
  • 300g minced meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 onion

How to Cook

  1. Boil spaghetti.
  2. Cook minced meat.
  3. Add sauce and spices.
  4. Serve hot.

Chapati with Beans Stew

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups beans
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tomatoes

How to Cook

  1. Prepare chapati dough.
  2. Cook chapatis.
  3. Cook beans stew.
  4. Serve while hot.

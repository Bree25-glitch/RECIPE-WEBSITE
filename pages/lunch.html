Lunch Recipes

Discover delicious and easy-to-make lunch recipes for every day of the week. From quick sandwiches to hearty salads, find your next favorite meal here!

Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Cooking cream
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

How To Cook

  1. Boil pasta until soft.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan.
  3. Add garlic and cream.
  4. Mix with pasta.
  5. Serve hot.

Beef Burger

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

How To Cook

  1. Cook beef patty.
  2. Toast burger buns.
  3. Add lettuce and tomatoes.
  4. Place cheese and beef patty.
  5. Serve with fries.

Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Cooking oil

How To Cook

  1. Heat oil in pan.
  2. Cook vegetables and eggs.
  3. Add rice.
  4. Pour soy sauce.
  5. Mix well and serve.

Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Vegetables

How To Cook

  1. Spread sauce on dough.
  2. Add cheese and toppings.
  3. Bake in oven.
  4. Cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Slice and serve.

Grilled Fish

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

How To Cook

  1. Season fish.
  2. Heat grill pan.
  3. Cook fish both sides.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice.
  5. Serve with vegetables.

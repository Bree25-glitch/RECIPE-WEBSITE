Lunch Recipes

Quick and tasty lunches to power your day.

Chicken Pasta

25 mins

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • Chicken breast
  • Cooking cream
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Boil pasta and cook chicken with garlic and cream.

Beef Burger

30 mins

Ingredients:

  • Burger buns
  • Beef patty
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook patty, assemble burger with toppings, and serve.

Fried Rice

20 mins

Ingredients:

  • Cooked rice
  • Vegetables
  • Eggs

Instructions:

  1. Stir-fry vegetables and eggs, add rice and sauce.

Homemade Pizza

40 mins

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese and toppings

Instructions:

  1. Top dough, bake until cheese melts and crust is golden.

Grilled Fish

25 mins

Ingredients:

  • Fresh fish
  • Lemon
  • Garlic and herbs

Instructions:

  1. Season and grill fish until cooked through; serve with lemon.
